Kaizoku Sentai Gokaiger

These six youths who sailed across the cosmic sea in pursuit of adventure and glory! Raising their flags against Space Empire Zangyack, the brilliant men and women who take pride in being called pirates! They are...

Captain Marvelous

Gokai Red

He desires everything to be stimulating, and he is always curious. Marvelous is the type to poke his head in where it does not belong, and he often brandishes his crew of 5 with unreasonable orders. However, he is also someone who cares much for his crew and for others even if he doesn't show it.

Joe Gibken

Gokai Blue

A calm man, whose words are few. A compliment from this man is rare, a man with a serious personality who is hard on himself. Always training up his abilities with weapons, and never misses. Has known Marvelous the longest, and as such, he understands Marvelous the best out of the entire crew.

Luka Millfy

Gokai Yellow

A tomboy and the ship's lookout who dislikes needing to have a reason to act. Excels in RECON and exploration, and has a strong tenacity for treasure hunting, earning her respect from her peers. However, her need to get the treasure often makes her forget the mission is in front of her, be that when it comes to the enemies, or her friends.

Don "Doc" Dogoier

Gokai Green

He is a pacifist who causes trouble because he wants to avoid fighting. When frightened, Don will cower behind either Luka or Joe, but mostly Luka, often resulting in an elbow to the stomach.
